Day 1: pull

  1. Deadlift 4 x (6-12)
  2. Barbell Rows 3 x (10-12)
  3. T-Bar Rows 3 x (10-12)
  4. Dumbbell Rows 3 x (10-12)
  5. Bicep Curls 4 x 12
  6. Seated Alternating Dumbbell Curls 3 × (10-12)
  7. EZ-Bar Bicep Curls on Bench 3 x (10-12)
  8. Cable Bicep Curls 4 × 12
  9. Overhead Shoulder Press 4 x (10-12)
  10. Dumbbell Shoulder Press supersetted with Dumbbell Front Raise 4 x (10-12)

Day 2: Leg

  1. Barbell Squats (5-6) x (2-12)
  2. Leg Press 4 x 12
  3. Lunges 2 sets of 30 m each
  4. Deadlift (either with straight or slightly bent legs) 3 x 6-12
  5. Leg Curls 3 x 12

Day 3: Push

  1. Bench Press 5 x 12
  2. Incline Bench Press 3 x 12
  3. Dumbbell Press 3 x 12
  4. EZ-Bar Triceps Extensions 3 x 12
  5. Seated Dumbbell Triceps Extensions 4 x 12
  6. Close Grip Bench Press 4 x 12

Day 4: Pull

  1. Barbell Rows 5 x (10-12)
  2. Low Pulley Rows 4 x (10-12)
  3. Close-Grip Lat Pulldowns 3 x (10-12)
  4. Wide-Grip Lat Pulldowns 3 x (10-12)
  5. Seated Alternating Bicep Curls 4 x 12
  6. Machine Bicep Curls supersetted with Barbell Bicep Curls 3 × 12/
  7. Low Cable Bar Curls 4 x 12
  8. Overhead Shoulder Press 4 x 12
  9. Front Raises 3 x (8-25)
  10. Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press 3 x (8-25)
  11. Lateral Raises 4 x 12

Day 5: Leg

  1. Leg Extension 4 x 30
  2. Front Squat 4 x (12-15)
  3. Hack Squat 3 x 12
  4. Standing Leg Curls 3 x (12-15)
  5. Leg Curls 4 x (12-15)

Day 6: CHEST, TRICEPS, CALVES AND ABS

  1. Dumbbell Incline Bench Press 4 x 12
  2. Decline Bench Press 3 x 12
  3. Incline Dumbbell Flvs 3 × 12
  4. Decline Dumbbell Flys 3 x 12
  5. Skullcrushers trisetted with Parallel Bar Dips trisetted with Seated EZ-bar Triceps
  6. Extensions 4 × 12/4 x 12/4 x 12
  7. Standing Calf Raises 4 x 12
  8. Crunches 4 × 12-15
