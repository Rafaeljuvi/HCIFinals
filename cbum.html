DAY 1: Back

  1. 4 sets of Deadlifts (10, 8, 8, going to failure on the 4th set)
  2. 4 sets of Bent-over Rows (12, 10, 8, 8)
  3. 4 sets of Lat Pulldown: (10 to 15 reps, failure on the last set)
  4. 4 sets of Straight Arm Lat Pulldowns (10 to 15 reps, failure on the last set)
  5. 4 sets of Dumbbell Row (15, 12, 10, 10 reps)
  6. 3 sets of Machine Row (20 reps or high volume)
  7. 2 sets of Hyper Extension, each to failure

Day 2: Chest and Biceps

  1. 5 sets of Incline Dumbbell Press (15, 15, 12, 12, and 10 reps)
  2. 4 sets of Bench Press (12, 10, 8, and 8 reps)
  3. 3 sets of Incline Flyes (15, 12, and 12 reps)
  4. 3 sets of Cable Flyes (15, 12, and 12 reps)
  5. 3 sets of Push Ups to failure
  6. 3 sets of Barbell Curls (12 to 15 reps)
  7. 3 sets of Preacher Curls (10 to 12 reps)
  8. 3 sets of Hammer Curls (10 to 12 reps)

Day 3: Glutes and Hamstrings

  1. 4 sets of Leg Curls (15 reps and last set to failure)
  2. 4 sets of Romanian Deadlifts (15 to 20 reps)
  3. 4 sets of Standing Leg Curls (8 to 10 reps)
  4. 4 sets of Reverse Hack Squats (15 to 20 reps)
  5. 3 sets of Glute Kickbacks (12 to 15 reps)

Day 4: Shoulders and Triceps

  1. 3 sets of Dumbbell Lateral Raises (15 reps)
  2. 3 sets of Dumbbell Shoulder Press (12 reps)
  3. 3 sets of Barbell Front Raise (12 reps)
  4. 4 sets of Single Arm Cable Raise (20, 15, 15, and 12 reps)
  5. 3 sets of Upright Rows (15, 12, and 12 reps)
  6. 3 sets of Face Pulls (15, 12, and 12 reps)
  7. 3 sets of Machine Lateral Raises (15 reps)
  8. 4 sets of Dips (12 to 15 reps)
  9. 4 sets of Skull Crushers (12 to 15 reps)
  10. 3 sets of Cable Kickbacks (12, 10, and 8 reps)

Day 5: Quads

  1. 3 sets of Leg Extensions (15 reps)
  2. 6 sets of Back Squats (10 to 12 reps)
  3. 4 sets of Leg Press (20, 20, 15, 12 reps)
  4. 4 sets of Standing Lunges (8 to 12 reps)
  5. 4 sets of Standing Calf Raises (10 to 12 reps)
  6. 4 sets of Hip Adductors (10 to 12 reps)
